About Us

Century Electric, based in Springfield, Missouri, is a pioneering force in the electrical industry, dedicated to delivering top-notch services and innovative solutions to meet the diverse needs of our clients. With a rich history spanning decades, our company has established itself as a trusted name synonymous with reliability, expertise, and excellence.

At Century Electric, we specialize in providing a comprehensive range of electrical services, including installation, maintenance, and repair for residential, commercial, and industrial properties. Our team of highly skilled electricians is committed to delivering superior craftsmanship and ensuring the highest standards of safety and efficiency in every project we undertake.

As a customer-centric organization, we prioritize building strong relationships with our clients by understanding their unique requirements and offering tailored solutions that exceed their expectations. Whether it's upgrading electrical systems, troubleshooting issues, or implementing energy-efficient solutions, we are dedicated to delivering results that enhance the comfort, safety, and productivity of our clients' spaces.

Driven by a passion for innovation and sustainability, Century Electric remains at the forefront of advancements in the electrical industry. We continuously invest in training and technology to stay ahead of the curve and provide our clients with cutting-edge solutions that meet the demands of today's ever-evolving world. With a commitment to integrity, professionalism, and excellence, Century Electric is your trusted partner for all your electrical needs in Springfield, Missouri, and beyond. We take pride in our work and strive to uphold our reputation as the premier choice for electrical services, delivering value-driven solutions that power the success of our clients and communities.
